What is TigerGrowl?





TigerGrowl makes setting up meals on the fly even easier through the use of indirect invitations. Broadcast to your Facebook friends on campus when and where you are getting a meal, or look through your Feed to see when others are eating.

Join

See when and where your friends are having meals around campus.

Host

Set up your own meals for all your friends to see and join.

Connect

Invite friends both old and new to meals you host and catch up over a meal.
